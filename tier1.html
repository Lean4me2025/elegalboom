Tier 1 – Startup & Essential Documents

Ideal for new entrepreneurs and small businesses who need a strong starting document without overcomplicating the process.

What Tier 1 Is For

Tier 1 is designed for simple but important agreements that protect your ideas, clarify money owed, or document your single-owner business structure.

Common Tier 1 Documents

Mutual NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)

Protects confidential business conversations while you explore ideas.

Promissory Note

Documents money loaned between people or businesses, with clear repayment terms.

Single-Member LLC Operating Agreement

Clarifies ownership, purpose, and management for a one-owner LLC.

Simple Payment / IOU Agreement

Creates a written record of what is owed and when it must be repaid.

Basic Contractor / Service Letter

Lightweight agreement confirming who is doing what, and for how much.
