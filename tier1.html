What Tier 1 Is For

Tier 1 is designed for simple but important agreements that protect your ideas, clarify money owed, or document your single-owner business structure.

Common Tier 1 Documents

Mutual NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)
For sharing ideas, plans, or information while keeping it confidential.
Promissory Note
Documents money loaned between people or businesses, with repayment terms.
Single-Member LLC Operating Agreement
Clarifies ownership, purpose, and management for a one-owner LLC.
Simple Payment or IOU Agreement
Basic written record of what is owed and when it must be repaid.
Basic Contractor/Service Letter
Lightweight agreement confirming who is doing what, for how much.
Other Basic Business Forms
Simple, single-purpose documents to support your startup.
