Tier 3 — Advanced Control & Growth Protection

Tier 3 is designed for established businesses that need stronger governance, clearer authority structures, and legal protection to support growth, investors, and long-term stability.

This tier focuses on control.
It formalizes decision-making, protects intellectual property, and prepares your business for scaling, audits, and outside scrutiny.

Included Documents

  • Amended & Restated Operating Agreement
  • Shareholder Agreement
  • Advanced Buy-Sell Agreement
  • Management Services Agreement
  • Intellectual Property Assignment
  • Non-Compete / Non-Solicitation Agreement
  • Corporate Resolution Templates
  • Officer Appointment & Authority Documents

What This Tier Solves

  • Clarifies who has authority — and when
  • Protects company IP and competitive position
  • Defines exit, transfer, and succession rules
  • Supports investors, lenders, and advisors
  • Reduces internal disputes as the company grows

Who This Is For

  • Multi-member LLCs or corporations
  • Businesses preparing for investment or acquisition
  • Companies formalizing management and officer roles
  • Founders transitioning from “startup” to “operator”
