Tier 3 is for businesses that are ready to operate with professional contracts, clear expectations, and stronger protection. This package gives you service-focused documents that help you work with clients and contractors in a clean, structured way.

Think of Tier 3 as your “working every day with clients and vendors” toolkit.

Who Tier 3 Is For

Tier 3 is a strong fit if you:

  • Provide services to clients (consulting, coaching, design, security, etc.)
  • Hire independent contractors and need clear agreements
  • Want written expectations for scope, payments, and responsibilities
  • Need a consistent way to protect your time, work, and relationships

What’s Included in Tier 3

Your customized Tier 3 package includes:

  • Service Agreement (Client-Facing)
    A clear, professional contract that defines the scope of services, payment terms, timelines, responsibilities, and boundaries when you work with your clients.
  • Independent Contractor Agreement
    Used when you are hiring contractors to help with your work. Clarifies that they are not employees, outlines pay and expectations, and helps protect your business from disputes.

What You’ll Need Ready

To complete Tier 3 quickly, you’ll want to have:

  • Your business name and contact information
  • Primary services you offer (for the Service Agreement)
  • Typical payment terms (up front, milestones, or after completion)
  • Basic contractor details if you’re hiring (name, role, pay structure)
  • State where you operate so the documents match your region
