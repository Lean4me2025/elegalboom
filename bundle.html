Entrepreneur Starter Bundle

For founders who know they’ll need multiple documents in a short window and want the best overall value.

Bundle Price: $149 flat + 30-day access

What the Bundle Is For

The Entrepreneur Starter Bundle is ideal when you’re forming, documenting, and hiring within the same 30-day window. One payment gives you access to prepare multiple key documents during that period.

What’s Included

Tier 1 Document Types
Access to Tier 1 document types such as NDA, Promissory Note, and Single-Member LLC Agreement.
Tier 2 Document Types
Partnership, multi-owner LLC, and other foundation agreements as your business structure expands.
Tier 3 Document Types
Premium services, consulting, and joint venture style agreements for higher-impact relationships.
One Intake per Document
Each document still receives its own focused intake, so the quality stays high even in bundle mode.

How It Works

  • Select the Entrepreneur Starter Bundle.
  • Complete a short bundle intake to set your 30-day window.
  • Choose which document you want to generate first.
  • Complete one intake per document during your access window.
  • Receive each customized document by email.
