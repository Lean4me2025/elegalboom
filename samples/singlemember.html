This Operating Agreement (“Agreement”) is made by the sole Member, [Member Name], for the purpose of governing the operations of [LLC Name], a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of [State].

1. Formation

The Member formed a Limited Liability Company (“LLC”) on [Formation Date] by filing Articles of Organization with the Secretary of State of [State].

2. Purpose

The purpose of the LLC is to engage in any lawful business activity permitted under state law, including but not limited to the activities described in the Member’s intake submission.

3. Sole Member Ownership

The LLC is owned 100% by [Member Name]. No other individuals or entities hold ownership or membership interests.

4. Capital Contributions

The Member has contributed the following capital to the LLC:

  • Cash: $[Amount]
  • Property: [Description]
Additional contributions may be added at the Member’s discretion.

5. Management

The LLC shall be **member-managed**. The Member has full authority to operate the business, enter contracts, manage finances, hire staff, and perform all necessary actions for the company.

6. Liability Protection

The Member is not personally liable for the debts, obligations, or liabilities of the LLC. The LLC is treated as a separate legal entity.

7. Profits & Losses

All profits and losses shall be reported as the Member’s personal tax responsibility and may be distributed to the Member at any time.

8. Records

The LLC shall maintain records including this Agreement, Articles of Organization, financial statements, and tax filings at the company’s principal office.

9. Dissolution

The LLC may be dissolved at the Member’s discretion. Upon dissolution, all assets will be distributed to the Member after debts have been satisfied.
