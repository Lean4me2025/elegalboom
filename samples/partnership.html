This Partnership Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into as of [Date], by and between the following Partners:

  • [Partner A Name]
  • [Partner B Name]

Collectively referred to as the “Partners,” and individually as a “Partner.”

1. Formation of Partnership

The Partners hereby form a general partnership under the name: [Partnership Name]. The Partnership is formed to engage in [Business Purpose] and any lawful activities agreed upon by the Partners.

2. Contributions

Each Partner contributes the following capital, property, or services:

  • Partner A: [Contribution Description]
  • Partner B: [Contribution Description]

Additional contributions may be made by mutual agreement.

3. Ownership & Profit Sharing

Profits and losses shall be shared according to the following percentages:

  • Partner A: [X%]
  • Partner B: [X%]

Allocations will be updated in the final customized agreement.

4. Management & Decision-Making

Each Partner has equal rights in the management and operations of the business unless otherwise specified. Major decisions — such as admitting new Partners, obtaining loans, or dissolving the Partnership — require approval by all Partners.

5. Duties of Each Partner

Each Partner agrees to:

  • Act in good faith toward the Partnership
  • Perform duties consistent with the business purpose
  • Avoid conflicts of interest
6. Banking & Records

A Partnership bank account shall be opened in the business name. The Partners will maintain accurate financial records that are accessible to each Partner at all times.

7. Withdrawal or Death of a Partner

If a Partner withdraws, becomes incapacitated, or dies, the remaining Partner may choose to continue the Partnership or dissolve it according to the final agreement terms.

8. Dissolution

The Partnership may be dissolved at any time by mutual consent. Upon dissolution, assets will be distributed after all debts and liabilities have been satisfied.
