4. Ownership, Profits & Losses

Ownership percentages determine each Member’s share of profits, losses, and distributions. These percentages will appear in your final customized Agreement.

5. Management Structure

The LLC is (choose one in intake): Member-Managed — Both Members actively run operations or Manager-Managed — A designated Manager oversees operations

Major decisions require approval of both Members unless otherwise stated.

6. Duties of Members

Each Member shall:

  • Act in good faith
  • Fulfill assigned operational roles
  • Avoid conflicts of interest
7. Voting Rights

Voting power corresponds to ownership percentages unless the Members choose equal voting in their intake selection.

8. Meetings

The Members will meet at least annually to review financial statements and major operational decisions. Additional meetings may be held as necessary.

9. Transfer of Interests

A Member may not transfer their ownership interest without the written consent of the other Member. Buyout provisions will be included in the final version based on your intake options.

10. Dissolution

The LLC may be dissolved upon mutual agreement of both Members or under conditions described in your final customized document. Upon dissolution, assets will be distributed according to ownership percentages.
