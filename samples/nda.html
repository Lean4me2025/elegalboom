Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (Sample)

Sample Content Only – Your completed document will be customized based on your intake answers.

1. Purpose

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (“Agreement”) is made for the purpose of preventing unauthorized disclosure of confidential information shared between the parties. Each party may disclose confidential information to the other for evaluating a potential business relationship or collaboration.

2. Definition of Confidential Information

“Confidential Information” means any non-public information disclosed by either party, including business plans, financial data, client information, product designs, processes, and any materials marked or reasonably understood to be confidential.

3. Obligations of the Receiving Party

  • Maintain confidentiality of the disclosed information.
  • Use the information solely for the evaluation of a business relationship.
  • Not disclose information to any third party without written consent.

4. Term

The obligations under this Agreement continue for **two (2) years** from the date of disclosure, unless otherwise specified in the final customized document.

5. Return of Materials

Upon request, each party will return or destroy all confidential documents and materials received from the other.

6. No License Granted

Nothing in this Agreement grants any rights, licenses, or ownership in the other party’s confidential information.

7. General Provisions

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the state indicated in your intake form. Any modifications to the Agreement will be reflected in your final customized version.
