BUSINESS LAUNCH KIT OVERVIEW

(Starter Bundle – Sample Preview)

This Launch Kit provides the essential foundation for establishing and operating a small business professionally and legally. The final version you receive from E-LegalBoom will be personalized based on your intake responses and the legal requirements of your state.

1. Business Identity Setup

Your Launch Kit includes guidance and templates for establishing your business identity, including:

  • Business Name Verification
  • Domain & Branding Recommendations
  • Basic Operating Description Template
2. Essential Documents Included

The Starter Bundle contains the core documents every small business should have in place:

  • Basic Service Agreement – Defines the work, boundaries, and payments
  • Basic Contractor Agreement – For hiring part-time and freelance support
  • Basic NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) – Protects sensitive information
  • Intake Worksheet – Ensures all information is collected clearly
3. Business Structure Guidance

We provide easy-to-understand guidance for selecting your business structure, including:

  • Sole Proprietorship
  • Single-Member LLC
  • General Partnership

You’ll receive state-specific filing instructions so you know exactly where to submit forms and what fees apply.

4. Compliance Checklist

The Launch Kit also includes a step-by-step startup checklist:

  • Required state filings
  • Suggested registrations (EIN, Seller’s Permit, etc.)
  • Recommended compliance reminders
5. Delivery & Format

Upon completing your intake, you will receive:

  • Your personalized documents (PDF and Word format)
  • Filing instructions based on your state
  • A Launch Roadmap that guides you through setup


This preview shows sample-only content. Your full Launch Kit will include detailed templates, instructions, and action steps customized specifically for your business.
