MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (SAMPLE) This Agreement is entered into between the Parties for the purpose of exploring a potential business relationship. Each Party agrees not to disclose confidential information received from the other Party, except as allowed in this Agreement. • "Confidential Information" includes non-public business, financial, technical, or strategic data. • Information must be protected and used only for evaluating the proposed business relationship. • At the end of discussions, each Party agrees to return or destroy confidential materials.
