Mutual NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)

Typical Tier: Tier 1
For confidential business conversations
Names, dates, and clauses are placeholders. Your document will be customized after purchase.
MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (SAMPLE) This Agreement is entered into between the Parties for the purpose of evaluating a potential business relationship. Each Party agrees not to disclose confidential information received from the other Party except as permitted in this Agreement. Confidential Information includes business, financial, technical, or strategic data. Information must be protected and used only for evaluating discussions. Upon termination, each Party agrees to return or destroy confidential materials.
