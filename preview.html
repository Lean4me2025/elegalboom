How We Deliver Your Documents

  1. Secure Payment: Complete checkout for your selected tier or bundle.
  2. Guided Intake: We send you a short, guided intake form to capture your names, percentages, addresses, and details.
  3. Document Assembly: We merge your information into our templates that match your state and entity type.
  4. Delivery in 24–48 business hours: You receive your completed documents by email in ready-to-use format (usually PDF + Word).
  5. DIY Filing: We provide clear written guidance on where to file, how to sign, and how to keep copies organized.
    State filing fees are paid directly by you to your state or agency.
