Tier 2 – Standard Legal Foundation

Tier 2 is built for businesses ready to put a true foundation under their operations. These documents strengthen your partnerships, define ownership, and protect your long-term structure. You receive state-aligned legal templates tailored through your intake form.

This tier takes you from “starting up” to “operating with protection and clarity.”

Who Tier 2 Is For

Tier 2 is ideal if you:

  • Are forming a multi-owner business (2 or more people involved)
  • Need clarity on roles, responsibilities, and ownership
  • Are entering a partnership requiring written legal structure
  • Want documents that help prevent disputes and protect everyone involved

What’s Included in Tier 2

Your customized Tier 2 package includes:

  • Partnership Agreement
    Defines the relationship, responsibilities, equity split, profit handling, decision-making, and exit procedures between partners for business protection.
  • Two-Member LLC Operating Agreement
    Outlines ownership percentages, management structure, financial contributions, voting rules, profit allocation, and dissolution terms for a 2-owner LLC.

What You’ll Need Ready

To complete Tier 2 smoothly, gather:

  • Legal names and emails of both owners/partners
  • Business name & formation state
  • Ownership percentages
  • Initial financial contributions
  • Roles & responsibilities for each partner
  • Voting structure (majority, unanimous, weighted, etc.)
