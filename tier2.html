Tier 2 – $59 — Business Formation & Core Agreements

Foundational legal documents to properly form, protect, and operate your business.

Documents Included

  • LLC Operating Agreement — Establishes ownership structure, authority, and internal governance.
  • Partnership Agreement — Defines partner roles, profit sharing, and decision rights.
  • Founder Agreement — Clarifies founder responsibilities, equity expectations, and commitments.
  • Buy-Sell Agreement — Sets rules for ownership transfers, exits, and unexpected events.
  • Independent Contractor Agreement — Defines service relationships and protects business interests.
  • Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) — Protects confidential business information.

What This Tier Is For

  • Forming a business with clear legal structure
  • Defining ownership and responsibilities
  • Protecting sensitive business information

Who This Tier Is For

  • New businesses and startups
  • Founders forming LLCs or partnerships
  • Businesses beginning operations or hiring contractors
